# Zenith Master Timeline (v0.3 -> v1.0)

This document organizes the evolution of Zenith into a single vision, now starting from the milestone where Ascension was completed as the official engine.

## 1. Current State: Zenith Ascension (v0.3.0)

Zenith is officially in its self-hosted phase.

- Status: self-hosted, operational, and documented
- Highlights: complete self-hosting, engine optimization, and multi-platform distribution
- Quality: integrated test suites via ZTest
- References: [Core](core.md) | [Ascension](ascension.md)

## 2. Next Cycle: Industrial Maturity (v0.4 -> v0.9)

Expansion of tooling, ergonomics, and productivity upon the already completed foundation.

- Focus: Compass, Atlas, Keter, and new layers of productivity
- Goal: broaden integration coverage, observability, and packaging
- Reference: [Quality Roadmap](quality.md)

## 3. Future Vision: Zenith v1.0 Odyssey

The path toward v1.0 continues looking at Zenith as a broader platform for UI, web, games, and automation.

- Status: planning
- Target Frameworks: Zenith Meridian, Zenith Borealis, Zenith Intersect, and Zenith Orion
- Reference: [Odyssey Vision](odyssey.md)
